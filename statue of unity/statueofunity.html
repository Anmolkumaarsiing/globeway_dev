Statue of unity

The Statue of Unity stands tall as a tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, a key figure in India’s struggle for independence and unity. At 182 meters, it's the tallest statue in the world, proudly overlooking the Narmada Valley in Gujarat. This impressive monument is not just a marvel of engineering but a symbol of national pride and unity. Visitors can explore its museum and exhibition hall, take in breathtaking views from its observation deck, and stroll through beautifully landscaped gardens. The Statue of Unity offers a powerful reminder of Patel’s vision and a chance to connect with India’s rich history.
